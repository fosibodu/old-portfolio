Currently a side project in progress, I am developing a concept mobile app of Way Bag, a product created by Eunie Kim for her senior design thesis. I did some product photography for her project which she used in her website.

As a twenty-something growing up in the digital age with smartphones, I have noticed that we as a society have shifted from shopping in stores to the web and now on our mobile phones and tablets. I want to create a functional and effective application which would bring ease to online shopping without the frustration that users tend to feel.

I am currently working through wireframes in Sketch. I am a beta tester for Adobe's Project Comet, which I will be using to create my prototypes.
