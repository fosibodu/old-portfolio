I was given the chance to put my design "skills" to the test when I volunteered myself to create The Daily Illini's Holiday Guide cover.

Anna, our Creative Director, put a lot of faith in me when I said I would design. It didn't help that this guide landed at the end of the fall semester and right before finals week. I had help from my two favorite graphic designers Scott and Bryan (he gave lots of necessary criticisms). The pattern was the most frustrating because it had to be symmetrical, in other words, perfect. I'm not officially calling myself a graphic designer just yet, I believe I still have a ways to go. Overall, I'm happy with what I've done.
